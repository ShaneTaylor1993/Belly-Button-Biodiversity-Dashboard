About The Visualization

This dashboard visualises bacterial samples of 153 volunteers's belly buttons. We've created three charts showing the top 10 bacterial species found in the belly buttons of the volunteers, bacterial species with the highest concentration per individual and belly button washing frequency per week for each volunteer. If Improbable Beef identifies a bacterial species that synthesizes proteins to taste like beef, our visualizations will help to identify if that species is found in the navel of the volunteers.
